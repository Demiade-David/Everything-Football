The Final of the UEFA Champions League

Everyone has been talking about the latest UCL semifinals. It was amazing, unpredictable and ended unexpectedly.

The semifinals(PSG vs Arsenal and Barcelona vs Inter Milan) ended with PSG and Inter Milan coming out victorious, with PSG beating Arsenal 3-1 and Inter Milan beating Barcelona in a narrow 7-6 lead.

Remarks From The Author About The Arsenal Match.
I personally thought that Arsenal would win that match no stress but Psg shocked me. A team that could beat Real Madrid 5-1 in aggregate, I thought the victory was sure. I have never been more wrong and now PSG advance to the finals.
About The Barcelona Match.
Barcelona really fell my hands when they lost but they cannot be blamed their star striker, Robert Lewandowski didn't play. Don't get me wrong I loved the match so many highlights and goals. But I was really expecting Barcelona to win. But good job to the Barcelona players, you guys did well.
